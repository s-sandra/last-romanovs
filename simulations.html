Citizens[]

Source Code Cameron Edmond 2017 # 13

Applicant no.3, Kent Persson, is applying for citizenship.
Kent Persson is of medium height.
Kent Persson is skinny.
Kent Persson has a third eye.
Kent Persson has grey eyes.
Kent Persson has no body hair.
Kent Persson has tanned skin.
Kent Persson speaks perfectly.
(...)
Kent Persson has 1289 funds to contribute to the nation's development fund.

During the interview, the applicant stated:
I am coming here due to a business opoortunity. I had a good life back home once, and I'd like to
recreate that here. I really just need a place to live, if I'm totally honest. There are some crimes on
my record, but they are from political protests.

Kent Persson is not suitable for citizenship.

REMEMBER, THE PERFECT CITIZEN:
is short.
is muscular.
There are currently 3 citizens in our beautiful nation.

“Citizens[]” narrates the application process of computer generated individuals seeking citizenship in a simulated society. Each time a citizen is admitted, the requirements for citizenship are amended to fit the new individual’s characteristics. By the end of the novel, only 21 people are admitted as citizens.

Choose Your Own Safari

Source Code Bilgé Kimyonok 2018 # 72

If we're lucky we'll see some big game today! Lions, elephants, maybe even leopards! But you know, when you go on a safari, you drive a lot through open spaces looking for the big game so fortunately to entertain yourself on the way you have the birds. I'm a birder myself and I'm sure we'll see many fascinating ones today!" He just finished his sentence when the ranger came back to the car to give him the signed permit to enter. You drive through the portal into this wild area. But where should you go first?

“Choose Your Own Safari” is an interactive fiction creating using Twine and PHP that simulates an East African safari, complete with background images.

The Infinite Fight Scene

Source Code Filip Hracek 2017 # 127

Briana moves her sword between herself and the orc. He leaps right into it. The sword pierces his flesh. He falls to the ground and screams in pain. Aren strikes down with his sword at the orc. But the orc is able to roll out of the way. Briana touches her temple and tries to implant confusion into the orc's mind. But she fails. The orc rolls, and puts his feet under him. He jumps at Briana.

This novel hacks the Choose Your Own Adventure game book “Insignificant Little Vermin” written by the author to remove interactivity and force the initial fight scene to continue on forever.

Sandra Shtabnaya | UMW Spring 2020 | CPSC 448
